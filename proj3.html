Conclusions¶

Initially, in the data wrangling plase,the dataset had 21 columns,the range of values was from 0 to 10865 ,Null and duplicate values were also present in some of the rows.¶

In the data cleaning phase,null values were handled,duplicated rows were dropped and wrong datatypes of columns were corrected:-¶

1. Columns namelytagline,homepage,keywords,imdb_id,cast,director,overview,genres and production companies had null values which were replaced by an approproate string.¶

2. There was one duplicate value which was dropped.¶

3. Datatype of release date was an object which was changed to datetime.¶

In Exploratory Data Analysis phase following two questions were answered:-¶

Ques 1. What are the top 3 genres which were most made in year 1997¶

Findings:-¶

There is a list of genres like Drama,History,Fantasy,Comedy,Documentary,Western,Mystery,Foreign,Horror,Music,Animation,Adventure,Crime,Family,Romance,TV Movie,unknown genres,Science Fiction,War', Action and Thriller.¶

Among which plots show and we observe that Comedy,Drama and Thriller are the top 3 genres in movies which were made in year 1997 and information to be noted here is, no Western genre movies were made in this year.¶

Ques 2. In which season, movies make most revenue on average¶

Findings:-¶

we observe there are 4 seasons namely winter,summer,rainy and autumn ,a pie chart depicted the average revenue grossed by movies in a particular season in which average revenues were as follows:-¶

Winter 2.638043e+07¶

Summer 5.692919e+07¶

Rainy 2.997981e+07¶

Autumn 5.786999e+07¶

therefore it was observed that autumn was the season which grossed highest average revenue among all the seasons.¶

Limitations-this analysis cannot predict that coming years would follow the same trend i.e highest average revenue will surely be grossed in autumn season.¶
